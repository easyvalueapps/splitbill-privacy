🔒 Privacy Policy - SplitBill

📅 Last updated: August 6, 2025
📱 Application: SplitBill - Easy Bill Splitting
📦 Package: com.easyvalueapps.splitbill
🏢 Developer: EasyValue Apps

🎯 Executive Summary

SplitBill is a bill splitting app that prioritizes your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device and no personal information is sent to external servers.

📊 Data We Collect

SplitBill collects only the data necessary for functionality:

  • Split calculations: Bill amounts, participants, tips (stored locally)
  • Calculation history: To facilitate reuse (stored locally)
  • User preferences: Theme (light/dark), language, default currency
  • App settings: To maintain your preferences between sessions
⚠️ Important: All this data stays exclusively on your device. We do not have access to or collect this information.

❌ Data We Do NOT Collect

SplitBill does NOT collect, store, or have access to:

  • ❌ Sensitive personal information (name, SSN, ID, address)
  • ❌ Location data (GPS, approximate location)
  • ❌ Real financial information (credit cards, bank accounts, passwords)
  • ❌ Phone contacts (address book, phone numbers, emails)
  • ❌ Photos or camera (feature disabled in version 1.0)
  • ❌ Browsing history or data from other apps
  • ❌ Unique device identifiers
  • ❌ Usage data or behavioral analytics

💾 Data Storage

🔐 Local Storage Policy:
  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No information is sent to external servers
  • We do not maintain cloud backups of your data
  • Data is stored in the app's protected directory
  • Other apps cannot access SplitBill's data

🤝 Data Sharing

ZERO SHARING POLICY:

  • ✅ We do NOT share data with third parties
  • ✅ We do NOT sell user information
  • ✅ We do NOT have partnerships for data sharing
  • ✅ We do NOT use advertising networks that collect data
  • ✅ We do NOT integrate with invasive analytics platforms
📤 Voluntary Sharing: The only "sharing" occurs when you choose to send results via WhatsApp, email, etc. In this case, only the formatted calculation text is shared through Android's native sharing system.

📱 App Permissions

SplitBill uses only the minimum permissions necessary:

✅ Active Permissions:

  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save calculation history locally
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To access saved history
  • INTERNET: For future updates (v1.1) - currently not used

❌ Permissions NOT Used:

  • ❌ Camera (disabled in v1.0)
  • ❌ Microphone
  • ❌ Location (GPS)
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ SMS/Phone
  • ❌ Calendar

🔄 Future Updates (v1.1)

📷 OCR Scanning (Planned for v1.1):
When we enable receipt scanning:
  • Images will be processed locally on your device
  • We will NOT send photos to external servers
  • Extracted text will stay only on your device
  • You can disable this feature at any time

🗑️ Data Deletion

How to delete your data:

  • Method 1: Uninstalling the app automatically removes all data
  • Method 2: Clear data in Android settings
  • Method 3: Use "Clear History" function within the app (when available)
🔄 Guaranteed Deletion: Since all data stays on your device, uninstalling removes 100% of information related to SplitBill.

👶 Protection of Minors

SplitBill is suitable for all ages:

  • ✅ Does NOT collect data from minors
  • ✅ Contains NO inappropriate content
  • ✅ Rated for Everyone (ESRB: E)
  • ✅ Educational tool for learning division and math

🌍 International Transfers

NOT APPLICABLE: Since all data stays locally on your device, there are no international data transfers.

Processing based on:
